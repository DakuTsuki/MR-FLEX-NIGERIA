Who We Are

Steco Production is a sport fitness promotion outfit whose utmost motive is to develop athletes and fitness enthusiasts. We collaborate with other fitness professionals, sports experts, and international federations to create a balanced fitness and health advancement for youth.

We are determined and committed to give the best to enable our youths have a place in the sport of Bodybuilding development. These also will create great opportunities for the youths to attain the height they have been inspiring for in their choosing career.

Our Vision & Mission

To positively position and develop our athletes to excel in their chosen career also to uplift bodybuilding, fitness related programmes, powersports and the youths to gain more slot and leadership positions in world of sports for the country to be adequately represented in bodybuilding and fitness championship.

Aim & Objectives

  • To raise champions
  • To give Nigeria a sustainable place in world fitness and bodybuilding ranking
  • To eliminate miscreants and touting associated with bodybuilding
  • To reckon and promote brands
  • To incorporate closer relationships within the fitness community
